Instructions

  • Your character will automatically move forward, and your task is to control their movement to avoid obstacles.Each obstacle requires precise timing to overcome

  • To jump, press the up arrow key, Timing is crucial! Jump at the right moment to clear obstacles and continue your journey.

  • Be careful not to jump too early or too late, as mistimed jumps will lead to failure. Keep practicing to master the perfect timing!

  • Along the way, you'll encounter power-ups and bonuses. Collect them to enhance your abilities, gain extra points, or unlock special abilities.

  • As you progress, the speed of the scrolling environment will increase, making the game more challenging.
